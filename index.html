About Me

A small introduction to myself and my eariler stage and how i ventured into programming.

My name is Mubarak Basha, and I am currently 17 years old. I am a native Android and embedded systems developer, with projects ranging from the Arduino to Raspberry Pi. I got into programming through my passion for electronics, where I love to build IOT devices, home automation systems, and other projects. My first introduction to programming was with MicroPython, where I learnt to implement advanced robotics projects using a Raspberry Pi. However, I later explored other programming fields and languages, starting with Java, which was my first programming language for mobile development. Eventually, I switched to Kotlin.

I am not just a learner; I actively engage with the community. Whenever I learn something new, I publish it in articles on Medium. The articles I create are of high quality and include custom images designed using Figma. Additionally, I consistently contribute to open-source projects and explore new technologies. You can check out my GitHub repository, where I showcase multiple apps built using the latest technologies including but not limited to, Jetpack Compose, Dependency Injection, Unit Testing, and layered architecture.

I am passionate about sharing my knowledge with others.
